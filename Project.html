Indian Cuisine

Indian cuisine consists of a variety of regional and traditional cuisines native to the Indian subcontinent. Given the diversity in soil, climate, culture, ethnic groups, and occupations, these cuisines vary substantially and use locally available spices, herbs, vegetables, and fruits.

Continental

Continental food is referred to the food eaten in European countries.Fresh herbs are widely used in continental recipes. Dairy products form an important part of continental dishes. The French, Italian and Spanish parts of Europe, all do 'continental' dishes which focus more on olive oil, wine, garlic and various herbs and spices.
